How We Work

A Process Built
on Precision

Every engagement follows a structured lifecycle from assessment to continuous performance review.

01

Site Assessment

Ground-level evaluation of the assigned area — mapping waste generation points, household density, road access, and local conditions to build an accurate operational foundation.

02

Survey & RFID Card Installation

Door-to-door surveying of all households and establishments, followed by issuance and installation of RFID cards to enable digital tracking of waste collection and ensure accountability at every point.

03

Implementation

Systematic deployment of vehicles, manpower, and equipment as per the approved plan — with quality controls at every phase to ensure timelines and operational specifications are met without deviation.

04

Operations

Day-to-day execution strictly as per tender conditions — covering collection schedules, route adherence, staff deployment, vehicle upkeep, and compliance with all municipal and regulatory requirements.

05

Reporting & Optimisation

Regular submission of performance reports to the concerned authority, with data-driven review cycles to identify gaps, improve efficiency, and align continuously with evolving project benchmarks.
